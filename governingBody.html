From the Desk of President

I feel pleasure in extending you a very warm welcome on behalf of the Board of Governors as well as the whole GANDHI MEMORIAL NATIONAL COLLEGE OF NURSING family to the new aspirants seeking admission in this institute, for structuring their career in Nursing. We have clearly laid down our vision, mission and values and efforts shall be made to utilize the infrastructural facilities and intellectual capital to achieve these goals and values. It shall be our endeavour to prepare nurses with a sound educational programme so that they may serve the institutions, patients with extra care and meet the basic health needs of the community.

We are conscious of the changing needs of society; hence, we shall emphasize on imparting education and training to the students beyond the prescribed syllabus to make them good human beings & professionals also. The curriculum will be flexible enough to keep pace with changes that take place from time to time in the field of nursing and patient care.

We live and work together as a family unit and hope that your stay with us will be more comfortable, fruitful and memorable.

Dr. Gurdev Singh

From the Desk of General Secretary

We believe that nursing occupies a unique and privileged position of influence and trust in efforts to improve human health. “Maintaining one’s health is a social responsibility. The balance of a nation depends on the health of the masses and the service rendered to the sick and needy. Only if a nation is healthy, can people contribute productively for social mobility. Besides medicine and surgery, the care and concern of the members of the nursing community, helps the patient recover rapidly. Nursing as a profession, has undergone several changes and continues to change, in order to incorporate the needs of society.” Our core values are excellence, collaboration, social responsibility, innovation, and leadership. Which provide us with the foundation to shape the future of caring and Health. Faculty and students, is collaborative partners in this effort.

GMN College of Nursing has set high standards and diligently maintains it. We provide an education program that enhances student’s knowledge, their skills and attitude to qualify as competent nursing professionals in today’s highly competitive and health-conscious society. We believe in value-based education and wish to serve society through our assets our students

Principal Message
“Education is the most powerful weapon which you can use to change the world.”

I believe that Nursing is not merely a profession; it is a divine call to serve humanity. Gandhi Memorial National College of Nursing Ambala Cantt strives to nurture and empower compassionate caregivers, nurse educators, and researchers. Through our academic programs, research initiatives, and hands- on clinical experiences, we are committed to empower our students with the skills, knowledge, and ethical values necessary to excel in the dynamic healthcare landscape.

Currently the institute offers G.N.M Diploma and B.Sc Nursing Degree programme in nursing discipline. Being the Principal of the institute, I can assure the newcomers that they will have healthy learning environment in the institute. As our society and organization is committed to quality education, the students of our college would surely acquire the knowledge, skills and attitude, which would make them competent professionals. Not only in professional life, but the college would also help them in their all-round development of personality to face the challenges of life. In the institute we always strive to provide the best to our students in academics as well co-curricular activities

I invite you to join us in our mission to make a positive impact on the health and well-being of individuals, families, and communities around the globe. Together, let us uphold the values of excellence, integrity, and compassion that define who we are as healthcare professionals.

Let us shape your career together……

Prof. Ramlakhan Mali
