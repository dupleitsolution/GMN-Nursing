why nursing?

Nursing is a noble profession that has been immortalized by saint women Florence Nightingale and Mother Teresa, who rendered invaluable service to mankind. There is a drastic increase in the worldwide population leading to more suffering of mankind. This necessitated improved and wider medical support through the establishment of more hospitals and allied industries.

The desperate need for nursing made nurses a backbone of the health sector. They play a vital role in the recuperation of patients. Nurse contributes to a strong workforce within the clinical health care industry. After the IT industry, it is the turn of Indian nurses to explore infinite job opportunities abroad. It is estimated that around 1.5 million Indian nurses are required in the U.S.A alone follow by a more or less equal number of opportunities in the U.K, Australia, New Zealand and European countries. A nursing career offers a wide variety of options in terms of schedules, locations and levels of responsibility. Another major aspect for nurses is high compensation. It has a bright and never-ending career, nationally and internationally.
