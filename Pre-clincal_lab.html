Pre clinical Science lab

Pre-clinical Science laboratory is designed to enable the students to acquire knowledge of the anatomical structure and functions, biochemical composition, and its alteration of the human body systems. This Lab also provides the opportunity for the students to identify microorganisms and practice infection control methods. Preclinical Science lab includes models of human organs and systems, specimens such as bones, skeletons, disarticulated bones and brain, heart etc., and other equipment such as compound microscopes, micro balances, and histological slides.

OBJECTIVE
  • To describe the anatomical terms and organization of the human body.
  • To describe the structure and function of the body systems.
  • To develop tissue handling and dissecting skills.
  • To appreciate students for their proficiency in skills and procedures.
