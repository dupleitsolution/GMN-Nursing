Maternal & Child Health Lab

The MCH (Maternal and Child Health) lab serves as a pivotal learning environment where students gain hands-on experience in caring for mothers, infants, and children. This specialized lab is designed to simulate real-world clinical scenarios, offering nursing students a safe space to practice their skills and develop confidence in providing care across the spectrum of maternal and paediatric health.

Moreover, the MCH lab serves as a space for faculty-led demonstrations, workshops, and debriefing sessions. Faculty members use these sessions to provide valuable feedback, reinforce key concepts, and facilitate reflective discussions, promoting continuous learning and improvement among students.

AIMS
  • To provide students with practical training and hands-on experience in dealing with aspects related to pregnancy, childbirth, and women's reproductive health.
  • To help students to develop and understanding of modern approaches to child care.
OBJECTIVES
  • Skill Development: The primary objective of the MCH lab is to facilitate the development and refinement of clinical skills specific to maternal and child health nursing. This includes skills such as prenatal assessments, labour and delivery support, newborn care, paediatric assessments, and family-centred care practices.
  • Clinical Competence: The lab aims to enhance students' clinical competence by providing opportunities for hands-on practice in a controlled environment. Through simulation-based learning experiences, students can safely apply theoretical knowledge to practical scenarios, thereby gaining confidence in their abilities to care for maternal and paediatric populations.
  • Critical Thinking and Decision Making: Another objective is to cultivate critical thinking skills and clinical decision-making abilities. By engaging in realistic patient scenarios, students learn to assess situations, prioritize interventions, and adapt their care plans based on changing patient needs, fostering a strong foundation for evidence-based practice.
FACILITIES

MCH laboratory with separate labour room provides a comprehensive and high-quality teaching with simulation mannequins to practice and demonstrate competency of selected obstetric skills. The MCH lab helps the students to acquire knowledge and hands on training in mother and new born care that are to be practiced in clinical settings. It is fully equipped with Pelvis, Fetal skull, various instruments, different kinds of models and mannequins, charts, play articles, flash cards and necessary articles for procedure demonstration like assessment of antenatal mother, intranatal and post-natal mother, newborn care and growth and development of all age groups.
